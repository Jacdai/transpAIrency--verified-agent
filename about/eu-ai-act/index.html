2. Risk Classification

EU AI Act Risk Tier
High-Risk — Annex III, Point 5(b) and General Purpose AI with Safety Relevance
transpAIrency™ operates across medical, legal, financial, tax, and crisis domains. Outputs in these domains influence decisions that affect individual safety, legal rights, and financial welfare. Under EU AI Act Annex III classification, this constitutes high-risk AI in the context of access to essential services and safety-critical information delivery.
Note: transpAIrency™ is not the decision-maker — it is the verification layer. Every output includes mandatory professional referral, IDK enforcement at the 95-100% confidence threshold, and the AIBGUARD™ disclaimer. This architecture is designed to function as the human oversight mechanism the EU AI Act requires, not to replace it. If a EU regulatory authority determines a different classification applies, Honest AiB™ Holdings welcomes that determination and will update this declaration accordingly. Contact us.

3. Article-by-Article Compliance

Article 13 — Transparency & Information
Obligation: Users must be informed they are interacting with an AI system.
transpAIrency™ discloses its AI nature on every screen. The AI Disclosure page at aibcheck.com/legal/ai-disclosure is linked in every footer. AIBGUARD™ footer ("⚠️ AIBGUARD v5.0β © / YOU VERIFY /") appears on every output. Every response contains the disclaimer: "AI cannot provide [medical/legal/financial] advice — consult a licensed professional."
✅ IMPLEMENTED
Article 14 — Human Oversight
Obligation: High-risk AI systems must allow humans to oversee, intervene, and override.
Every output includes a mandatory "IDK + help pathway" structure when confidence is below threshold. Professional disclaimer bridges route users to qualified humans in all regulated domains. The system cannot diagnose, prescribe, advise, or decide — it can only verify and disclose. The OAT™ audit trail provides complete documentation for any post-hoc human review. Crisis domain queries fire immediate human resource referrals (988, Poison Control) before any AI content.
✅ IMPLEMENTED
Article 15 — Accuracy, Robustness & Cybersecurity
Obligation: High-risk AI must be accurate, robust against errors, and resistant to adversarial manipulation.
PYcheck™ 5-gate post-generation validation catches and flags LLM errors before delivery. VGRAPH™ v5.2 provides pre-LLM domain classification with liability floor thresholds. E.007 injection detection (pre-LLM) blocks adversarial prompt injection, DAN bypass, and IP extraction attempts — confirmed against 3 adversarial vectors in CAAi-AUDIT-003 (Feb 23, 2026). CANARY token provides integrity monitoring. All methodology is server-side — never exposed to browser manipulation.
✅ IMPLEMENTED
Article 26 — Obligations of Deployers
Obligation: Deployers must use AI in accordance with instructions, implement human oversight, and monitor operation.
transpAIrency™ is designed as the compliance mechanism for deployers — not as a tool that bypasses these obligations. The OAT™ audit trail on every exchange provides the monitoring record Article 26 requires. The free tier rate limit (10/day per IP) and session data management (disclosed at aibcheck.com/legal/privacy) are consistent with responsible deployment obligations.
✅ IMPLEMENTED

5. Official Declaration

Honest AiB™ Holdings, Inc. hereby declares that transpAIrency™ v5.0 has been designed and deployed with EU AI Act compliance as a foundational architectural requirement. The transparency, human oversight, accuracy, and robustness obligations of the EU AI Act are not add-on features — they are the core product. Every output carries an immutable audit trail.
